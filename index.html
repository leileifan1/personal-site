The thesis

AI transformation has two unfinished jobs.

First, someone has to translate messy operational pain into a useful AI-enabled artifact. Then someone has to make that artifact understandable, governed, and repeatable inside the organization.

My work sits between those two jobs: AI Development and AI Enablement.

01

AI Development

Turning ideas, documents, and workflows into practical AI prototypes that teams can actually test.

02

AI Enablement

Designing the governance, training, stakeholder alignment, and change rhythm that make AI usable beyond the demo.

Services

Where I can help.

I am most useful where AI ideas need both a working shape and an adoption path.

  2. 02 AI adoption enablement

  3. 03 PMO and governance support
